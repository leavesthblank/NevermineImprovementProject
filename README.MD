An improvement project of Nevermine(Advent of Ascension)

````
"How to make a good Nevermine mod: Remove anything Xolova and Tslat made and you will get a good mod."
````

## Changes to original mod(up to 3.0)

- Remove some shitty mobs and ALL shitty fucking "abilities" like pulling up players and taking shit or throw used tampons at your screen 
- Remove statues, banners, soul items, essence, creation slabs
- Remove resources bars and four useless gods
- Remove all mob damage immunity
- Remove ancient cavern
- Rollback irrelevant and annoying nerf Tslat has made
- Partially remove shitty skills requirement (Will get removed with other a huge load of fucking shit Xolova has made)
- adds many output of extraction device depending on dimensions with diamond bowl durability increases
- Greatly increase weapon drop rate and quantities of mobs
- Change mob drops and some of mob spawning dimension close to AoA3
- Change overworld ores spawning rate more like AoA3
- Lyndamyte armor will multiply player's damage by 1.5 and all armor effects only require wearing one piece
- Add recipes of worn book and all kinds of candy used for crafting treat bag(Currently Realmstone obtained by opening worn book in prerequisite dimension e.g. opening worn book in Deeplands will drop Crystevia and Iromine Realmstone)
- Summoning bosses won't consume spawning items
